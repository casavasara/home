Dreams

Currently cooking:

Simple color matching activity. Few days ago we brought home a copy of 'Pink Lemon' by Herv√© Tullet from our local library. The book is a collection of illustrations of various objects, all in mismatched colors. Like the title implies, a pink lemon is one of these objects. Inspired by the enthusiasm that our little one had for the book, I started sketching out a small interactive game based on the same concept.

Objects are presented in an unusual color (red banana, blue cat) with 3 alternative colors presented. When the correct color is matched to the item, the illustration changes color and audio is played (with the next puzzle following the same model).
